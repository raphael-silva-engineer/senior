Highlighted Industrialization Projects

Technology Transfer & Standardization (ZF)

The Goal: Solve a critical downtime issue in a highly contaminated environment and establish a new industrial standard for UV curing processes.

My Role & Outcome: I engineered a novel UV LED curing system that significantly improved process reliability. The solution was so successful it was adopted as an industrial blueprint and replicated across three production lines over three years (2019-2021). This multi-year initiative required managing a complex technology transfer, including close collaboration and system integration with a German equipment supplier to ensure seamless deployment.

Production Line Revamping (Supergasbras)

The Goal: Modernize a gas cylinder painting line to eliminate a major production bottleneck, reduce operational costs, and improve final product quality.

My Role & Outcome: I led the project to replace traditional curing ovens with a custom automated UV system. This revamping initiative successfully eliminated the bottleneck, reduced energy consumption by 45%, and significantly improved the paint hardness and durability, demonstrating a clear return on capital investment.

New Product Industrialization (Autonomous Robot)

The Goal: Take an innovative concept for an autonomous disinfection robot and manage its development into a fully deployable, pharma-compliant commercial product.

My Role & Outcome: I led the industrialization process from concept to final product, managing the complex integration of an AMR platform, LIDAR, vision systems, and safety sensors. The result was a market-ready product successfully commissioned for major clients like Novo Nordisk, showcasing my ability to bring new technologies from prototype to sustained in-the-field operations.

Capacity Increase & Automation (Astergraf)

The Goal: Solve a critical production bottleneck in a high-speed offset press to increase overall manufacturing capacity and reduce material loss.

My Role & Outcome: I designed and built a custom, ultra-compact UV curing module to operate in an extremely constrained space. This targeted automation project successfully resolved the bottleneck, directly enabling increased production rates and improved quality, demonstrating a clear impact on manufacturing OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness).
