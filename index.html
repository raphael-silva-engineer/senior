Highlighted Industrialization Projects

Technology Transfer & Standardization (ZF)

The Goal: Resolve recurring downtime caused by contamination in a high-volume production environment and establish a more robust and repeatable curing process.

My Role & Outcome: Designed and industrialized a UV LED curing system focused on reliability, maintainability, and manufacturability. The solution significantly reduced downtime and was validated for production use, becoming a standardized blueprint replicated across three production lines between 2019 and 2021. Worked closely with manufacturing teams and a German equipment supplier to ensure seamless integration and production readiness.

Production Line Revamping (Supergasbras)

The Goal: Eliminate a major production bottleneck while improving product quality and reducing operating costs.

My Role & Outcome: Led the mechanical design and industrialization of a custom automated UV curing solution to replace conventional ovens. The new system increased line throughput, reduced energy consumption by approximately 45%, and improved coating durability and consistency. Strong emphasis was placed on DFM, ease of maintenance, and stable operation under continuous production conditions.

New Product Industrialization (Autonomous Robot)

The Goal: Transform an innovative autonomous robot concept into a fully deployable, pharma-compliant commercial product.

My Role & Outcome: Owned the product from concept through production-ready deployment, coordinating mechanical design, system integration, supplier components, and validation activities. The final product was successfully commissioned at major industrial and pharmaceutical clients, including Novo Nordisk, demonstrating the ability to bring new products from prototype to sustained field operation.

Capacity Increase & Automation (Astergraf)

The Goal: Remove a critical bottleneck in a high-speed offset printing press to increase manufacturing capacity and reduce material loss.

My Role & Outcome: Designed a compact UV curing module capable of operating within extremely constrained space, considering tight tolerances and production safety requirements. The solution enabled higher production rates and measurable improvements in Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE), with a strong focus on manufacturability and long-term reliability.
